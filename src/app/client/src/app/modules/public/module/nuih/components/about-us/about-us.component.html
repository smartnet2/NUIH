National Urban Innovation Hub

NUIH is an initiative of the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs to support the development of cities across India and to create a resource-rich ecosystem of learning, sharing and disseminating for city managers and primary stakeholders in the urban transformation of India.

Our Vision

Providing a horizontal learning and knowledge sharing platform for exchange between cities, practitioners, academia, researchers and technologists;

Our Goal

Providing a horizontal learning and knowledge sharing platform for exchange between cities, practitioners, academia, researchers and technologists;
